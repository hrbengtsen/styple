### API Reference

#### Heading

A styled component with `Text`'s styles and variants. However, it defaults to `color="heading"` and `size` is chosen depending on heading tag unless overridden. Custom props:

  <Tbody>
    <Tr>
      <Th>
        <Code>as</Code>
      </Th>

        <Code color="normal">string</Code>

        </Popover>
      </Td>
      <Td><Code color="normal">"h1"</Code></Td>
      <Td><Text>Choose heading tag to display. Depending on tag different `size` variants are set.</Text></Td>
